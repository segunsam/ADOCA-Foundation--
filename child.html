About Us

Our programs and initiatives are designed to address the specific needs of our beneficiaries. We provide education and vocational training to orphans, so they can acquire the skills they need to become self-sufficient and contribute to their communities. We also provide healthcare services, counseling, and social support to persons living with disabilities, so they can lead fulfilling and meaningful lives. ADETORO OMOOBA CARES HOME (ADOCAH) is a non governmental, non political, not - for – profit making and private organization (NGO) which put the welfare of the poor, the rejected, the motherless, physically and mentally challenged and the less-fortunate ones in our society as its paramount interest. It is meant and solely established to cater, relief, rehabilitate, reform and to solicit assistance from public spirited individuals, institutions, private organizations and government for the less privileged ones without prejudice as regard to colour, tribe and religion.
